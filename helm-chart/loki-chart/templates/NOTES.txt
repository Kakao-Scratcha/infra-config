Loki has been deployed successfully!

To access Loki:
1. Check the Loki services:
   kubectl get svc -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} | grep loki

2. Access Loki via port-forward:
   kubectl port-forward -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} svc/loki-gateway 3100:80

3. Loki will be available at: http://localhost:3100

The Loki service is configured with proxy settings for external access.
